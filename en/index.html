Agriturismo Podere La Cavallara

Your stay surrounded by nature, relaxation and privacy

Nestled in the peaceful countryside, just a few minutes from Montefiascone and Lake Bolsena, Podere La Cavallara is an exclusive retreat where nature, elegance and tranquility come together.

Surrounded by a lush valley opening out toward the lake, the estate welcomes its guests into a private and authentic setting, easily accessible yet far from any noise — the perfect place to unwind and recharge in complete privacy.

The property features six independent apartments, thoughtfully designed and finished with attention to detail to ensure comfort, privacy and autonomy, accommodating up to ten guests per unit. Each apartment is fully equipped with a kitchen, linens, and everything needed for a carefree stay.

Enhancing the experience is a large swimming pool with a dedicated relaxation area, beautifully maintained gardens, fruit trees, willows and cypresses, along with a covered and shaded parking space available for guests.

Local Products

Farming & Experiences

Mr. Domenico is always available to assist guests throughout their stay, offering insights into how the farm is run, including demonstrations and, at times, hands-on experiences with agricultural equipment. He is also happy to share recommendations on where to source local food products such as fresh fish, eggs, traditional cured meats, honey, olive oil and wine, as well as suggest unique places to visit in the surrounding area. Organic fruit and vegetables can be sourced directly from nearby local producers, just a short walk away, as they are located in the immediate surroundings of Podere La Cavallara.
